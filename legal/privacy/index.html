Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026 (draft v0.1)

1. Two roles, stated plainly

For the Software’s own users (your team’s accounts, usage of the dashboard) we are the data controller. For the business-contact data you bring into the service (your prospects, your CRM records) we are a processor acting on your instructions under the Data Processing Agreement — that data’s privacy story is governed by the DPA, not this page.

2. What we collect from Software users

  • Account: name, work email, role/entitlements set by your organization.
  • Usage: sign-ins, approvals and decisions you take (these form the audit trail your organization relies on), and basic diagnostics.
  • The demo collects none of this — demo personas run entirely in your browser (localStorage); no account is created and no analytics identify you.

3. What we do with it

  • Operate the Software: authentication, entitlements, the approval/audit trail.
  • Security: detecting misuse, protecting tenant isolation.
  • We do not sell personal data, run advertising trackers in the product, or use your data to train AI models.

4. AI processing

The service uses large language models (Anthropic Claude) to draft and analyze content. Client data processed this way is sent under our processor agreements and is not used by us or configured to be used by the model provider to train models. Every consequential AI action requires human approval and is audit-logged.

5. Isolation and security

Customer data is isolated per organization at the database layer (row-level security), credentials are held by reference in a managed secrets store, and access is per-seat and logged. Full posture: the Trust Center in the app.

6. Retention and deletion

Account and audit records are retained for the agreement term plus the retention period in the DPA (audit ≥12 months, as your compliance record). At offboarding you receive a complete export; deletion follows the DPA schedule.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA and similar), you may have rights of access, correction, deletion, and portability over your user data. Write to luis@scalemethods.com; we respond within the statutory window. For prospect data processed on a client’s behalf, we route requests to the responsible client per the DPA.

8. Changes

Material changes will be notified in-app and by email to account owners.
